Hi!

Tim Berners Lee is a Software Engineer who invented the Internet (or World Wide Web) in 1989 while at CERN, the European Particle Physics Labratory. He is director of the World Wide Web Consortium that he founded and a Professor in the Computer Science Department at the University of Oxford.
